About fittedbykhomiiee

Founded in 2023, fittedbykhomiiee is a South African clothing brand dedicated to creating minimalist, high-quality essentials that stand the test of time. Our pieces are designed to be versatile, comfortable, and effortlessly stylish.

Sustainable Materials

We prioritise eco-friendly fabrics and ethical production methods.

Timeless Design

Our pieces are designed to be wardrobe staples that never go out of style.

Quality Craftsmanship

Every item is crafted with attention to detail and built to last.
